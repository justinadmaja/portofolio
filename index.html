About Me

I was born in Balikpapan in 2002. Right now, I am still an undergraduate at BINUS University majoring in Computer Science. I want to broaden my experience and sharpen my skills as I feel I'm currently very lacking. But I'm confident in my ability to keep learning and adapt.

From my teenager years, I always liked programming more than other subjects, with that and adding my past love for playing games, I make games as a hobby and successfully published 2 games for now. 1 on itch.io and 1 on steam. With that I learned a lot of things like OOP programming, sound design, photoshop, pixel arts, steam, and most importantly experience from past mistakes. Currently still working alone in my next game project as a hobby to further improve my skills and materialize them to a game.

I like doing things ahead of schedule. With that I always finish up my work if possible way ahead of the time limit so I have a lot of time to review, fix up my work and rest without worries. With that I don't like people who are slow on their feet yet are not trying to improve on it. I also keep up to shape by doing regular exercises with a schedule and eat healthy to feel confident.

My Portofolio

Although these may not be all of my works as some needed a specific software to see (Ex. Android Studio), but these are the works that I wanted to feature as they are some of my proudest works.

Lake of Shadows (Solo)

A 2D pixel art side-scrolling resource management with some action, looting, and a bit of exploration that mainly focuses on a small area. Defend yourself against the terror of the night inside your settlement by employing some villagers and give them equipment to further strengthen your defenses.

Dungeon of Karma (Solo)

Random Generated Dungeon, Top-Down, Pixel-Art, Roguelike, Looting game. Welcome to the Dungeon of Karma, where you build up karma and spend them for permanent upgrades in this roguelike random generated dungeon. Loot, shoot and slash your way through the dungeon to get to the boss room from multiple playthroughs

FitnessZ (Solo)

An application design for a fitness app that help users to manage their time, activities and diet. It comes with some training courses and videos, activity tracker like sleep, calories, steps and water, with calories calculator and food recipes.

Danbam Website (Group)

A website design for a korean cafe. It comes with the menu of the website, locations of the branches, a way to contact them and order food online. I specifically worked on all of the website aspects including the designs, coding and art (logo).

Beaute Application (Group)

An online fashion store application design that is intended for girls and comes with some unique features. One of them is a camera function that captures a person's body and recommends clothing that fits them. It also comes with a very detailed pages for a work made using Figma.
